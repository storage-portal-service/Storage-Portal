Recommended downloaders

These tools work well with cloud and premium link services. Click a link to open the official site in your browser.

JDownloader 2

Free, open-source download manager. Supports many hosters, captcha handling, and queue management. Cross-platform (Windows, macOS, Linux).

Free Download Manager (FDM)

Free and full-featured: torrent support, browser integration, scheduling. Available for Windows, macOS, and Android.

Open FDM website

Internet Download Manager (IDM)

Windows-only, paid. Very fast and reliable for HTTP/HTTPS with strong browser integration and resume support.

Popular in China for P2P and HTTP downloads. Good for large files and various protocols. Windows and mobile.
